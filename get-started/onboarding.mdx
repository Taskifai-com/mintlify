Getting started is fast. Most brands are live within a day of connecting their first data sources.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Book a demo">
    A 30-minute call to see the platform and your use case. No commitment. [Book here](https://taskifai.com/book-demo).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign the mutual NDA">
    We execute a mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement before any data integration. Your reseller data stays confidential.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your first reseller sources">
    A 45-minute technical kickoff to connect your data sources. Send us your reseller reports in whatever format they arrive (XLS, CSV, PDF).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    The universal parser cleans, maps, and unifies your data. Within about 24 hours your dashboards are live and the AI advisor is ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The Strategic Partnership Pilot

New brands typically start with a 3-month guided pilot.

- **0 EUR monthly subscription** and **0 EUR setup fee** for 90 days
- Bi-weekly 30-minute syncs to calibrate the platform to your data
- Cancel any time with two weeks' written notice before the pilot ends
- Converts automatically to a paid tier based on your active reseller count unless cancelled
