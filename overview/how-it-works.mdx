TaskifAI turns fragmented reseller reports into strategic intelligence through three layers.

## Layer 1 — The Foundation (Automated Ingestion)

The universal parser ingests any reseller format: XLS, CSV, or PDF. It auto-detects column structures, standardises SKU names, and maps currencies. Zero manual entry.

<Note>
**95% reduction in data processing time.** Setup takes about 24 hours.
</Note>

## Layer 2 — The Intelligence (Unified Dashboard)

A single dashboard merges wholesale sell-out with Shopify DTC performance.

- Regional benchmarking across distributors and territories
- SKU-level sell-through rates and Weeks of Supply (WOS) calculations
- Stockout alerts and replenishment gap detection
- 10 pre-built KPI dashboards (Revenue, Sell-through, Inventory)
- Multi-currency support with automatic exchange rate conversion

## Layer 3 — The Insight (Natural Language AI Advisor)
