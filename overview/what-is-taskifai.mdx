TaskifAI automates the ingestion, cleaning, and unification of wholesale reseller sell-out data for beauty and fragrance brands, and adds a Natural Language AI Advisor on top.

## The problem: the distribution data gap

Brands know what they ship (sell-in). They are blind to what consumers actually buy (sell-out). Once product leaves the warehouse and enters the retailer's hands, visibility disappears.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="The monthly data grind" icon="clock">
    Operations teams spend 25+ hours a month reconciling spreadsheets. Every partner (Sephora, Douglas, Liberty, Selfridges, Harrods) reports in a different format. Manual SKU matching. Version control chaos.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Flying blind on inventory" icon="eye-off">
    Brands can see last month. They cannot see next week. Stockouts happen before anyone can react. Overstock ties up capital. Reactive decisions cost six figures a year.
  </Card>
  <Card title="The intelligence gap" icon="brain">
    Generic BI tools show graphs but don't explain why or what to do. Circana and NielsenIQ cost 50,000 EUR+ and need data analysts to extract value.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who it's for

TaskifAI is built for European prestige beauty and niche fragrance brands distributing through wholesale partners.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Ideal fit" icon="check">
    - European prestige beauty or niche fragrance brand
    - 5 to 100+ wholesale resellers
    - Revenue approx. 2M EUR to 50M EUR
    - Spending 20+ hours a month on manual sell-out reconciliation
  </Card>
  <Card title="Not the fit" icon="x">
    - Mass-market CPG brands
    - US-only brands
    - Brands with fewer than 5 resellers
    - Enterprise brands already on Circana contracts
  </Card>
</Columns>

## What makes it different

TaskifAI is the only platform combining real-time wholesale sell-out analytics with an AI advisor, purpose-built for European prestige retail, at a price point accessible to growth brands.
