## The challenge

As BIBBI's retail footprint grew, so did the complexity of its reporting. Each retail partner delivered sell-out data in different formats, structures, and levels of detail. There was no unified view of performance across SKUs, stores, or time periods.

> "The biggest challenge was that all our customers reported in different ways, which made it difficult to get a collective picture of our sell-out performance. We lacked the possibility to easily compare results between months and years and to analyze development per SKU."
>
> — Stina Seger, Founder, BIBBI Parfum

The team spent multiple days each month collecting, cleaning, and restructuring data, leaving little time for analysis and forward-looking decisions.

## The solution

BIBBI partnered with TaskifAI to centralize and standardize sell-out data from all retail partners. TaskifAI transformed disparate Excel files into a single structured dataset, making performance analysis consistent and repeatable across time periods, SKUs, and stores.

- **Data centralization:** aggregated data from all retail touchpoints into one system
- **Standardization:** converted varying retailer formats into a unified structure
- **Granular analysis:** performance tracking at both SKU and store level
