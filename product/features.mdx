Turn messy reseller spreadsheets into clean, trustworthy sales analytics, and ask your data anything in plain English.

## Get your data in, effortlessly

- **One-click reseller uploads** — Drop in any retailer's CSV or Excel file; TaskifAI auto-detects the format and parses it for you.
- **Works with every retailer format** — Pre-built support for Liberty, Selfridges, Harrods, CDLC, Aromateque and more, plus a no-code template builder for any new reseller.
- **Review before it counts** — Preview and validate every upload in a staging step, so only clean data reaches your dashboards.
- **It learns as you go** — Map a product or store once and TaskifAI remembers it, auto-matching future uploads.
- **Live store integrations** — Connect Shopify and WooCommerce to sync online orders automatically.

## See your whole business at a glance

- **Executive dashboard** — Total sales, YoY growth, units, average order value, margin and refund rate in one view.
- **Build-your-own dashboards** — Drag-and-drop widgets to create the views your team actually needs.
- **Store-level performance** — Drill into any store's revenue, trend, channel mix and top products.
- **Specialized KPI views** — Ready-made breakdowns by reseller, geography, channel, product velocity and margin.
- **Transaction ledger \+ export** — Browse, filter and export every transaction to CSV for your own reporting.

## Ask Mira, your AI analyst

- **Plain-English questions** — "What was my top product in France last quarter?" Mira writes the query and answers instantly.
- **Charts on demand** — Get visualizations generated automatically from your questions.
- **Remembers the conversation** — Ask follow-ups naturally; Mira keeps the context.
- **Context-injected insights** — The AI doesn't just look at numbers; it understands your specific business goals, product categories, and market positioning to provide strategic recommendations.

## Stay ahead of what's next

- **Sales forecasting** — Project demand months ahead per product, reseller or store.
- **Automatic anomaly alerts** — Get flagged on revenue spikes, declines and unusual refund rates before they become problems.
- **Trend detection** — Spot rising and declining products early.

## Reports that arrive on their own

- **Scheduled email digests** — A polished monthly summary of your key metrics and highlights, delivered automatically.
- **Reseller upload reminders** — Auto-nudge resellers who haven't sent their latest numbers.
- **Branded exports** — Generate PDF, Excel and CSV reports from any view.

## Your data, organized and yours

- **Product, store & reseller catalogs** — One clean master record set, with smart de-duplication and merge tools.
- **Secure & private by tenant** — Your data is fully isolated, with role-based access and multi-factor authentication.
