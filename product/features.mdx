## Automated data pipeline & multi-vendor ingestion

- **Zero-touch processing:** Automatically ingest, clean, and normalize sales data from fragmented spreadsheets (CSV, XLSX, XLS).
- **Auto-vendor detection:** Recognizes file structures from major resellers (Liberty, Selfridges, Galilu) without manual mapping.
- **Multi-store intelligence:** Extracts and flattens store-level data from complex master files for granular regional insight.

## Business-aware AI chat assistant

- **Natural language querying:** Ask complex sales questions in plain English. No SQL or Excel formulas required.
- **Context-injected insights:** The AI understands your business goals, product categories, and market positioning to give strategic recommendations.
- **Conversational memory:** Have a real-time dialogue with your data, with deep-dive follow-up questions.

## Unified multi-channel analytics

- **Single source of truth:** Consolidate offline B2B reseller data and online D2C (Shopify, WooCommerce) into one dashboard.
- **Comparative performance:** Side-by-side analysis of retail vs ecommerce to see which channels drive growth.
- **32+ specialized widgets:** KPI cards, trend charts, geographic heatmaps, top-product matrices.
- **Custom date range filtering:** Instant cross-platform filtering for precise period-over-period analysis.

## Operational excellence & reporting

- **Automated error management:** Row-by-row error reporting that pinpoints exactly where data is broken (e.g. "Invalid EAN" or "Missing Price").
- **Real-time status tracking:** Live progress monitoring for high-volume uploads.

## Enterprise foundation

- **Multi-tenant architecture:** Complete data isolation with database-per-tenant security.
- **MFA & secure access:** Multi-Factor Authentication (TOTP) and subdomain-based routing.
- **Role-based access control:** Manage team permissions so the right people see the right data.
