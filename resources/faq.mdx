<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does setup take?">
    About 24 hours from when you connect your first reseller data sources. The technical kickoff itself takes around 45 minutes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What file formats can you ingest?">
    Any reseller report format. The universal parser handles XLS, CSV, and PDF, auto-detects column structures, standardises SKU names, and maps currencies. No manual mapping required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is my data used to train AI models?">
    No. Your data is processed in isolated sessions via the Anthropic enterprise API and is never used to train any AI model. See our [Security page](/trust/security) for detail.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where is my data stored?">
    In EU-based data centers (DigitalOcean Germany and Netherlands) for full GDPR compliance. TaskifAI is headquartered in Malmö, Sweden.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can you connect both my wholesale and Shopify data?">
    Yes. TaskifAI merges offline B2B reseller data and online D2C data (Shopify, WooCommerce) into a single dashboard so you can compare channels side by side.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you charge a setup fee?">
    No setup fees. Billing is month-to-month, or annually with a 15% discount. You can cancel anytime.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is my pricing tier decided?">
    By the number of active resellers connected. Growth covers up to 15 resellers; Scale covers unlimited. See [Pricing](/get-started/pricing).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can you sign an NDA?">
    Yes. We execute a mutual NDA before any data integration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get the DPA or your SOC 2 report?">
    Email security@taskifai.com and we'll share the DPA, SOC 2 Type II report, and ISO 27001 certificate.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
